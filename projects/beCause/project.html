    • A social network for the causes you care about

    BeCause is a social network and discovery application that enables users to contribute to non-profits/charitable organizations they support in a more accessible and transparent way. Unlike other social networks, BeCause’s platform revolves strictly around the participation in volunteer, education, and donation opportunities, leaving no room for overused buzzwords and empty displays of support.

    I joined forces with two designers while focusing on user research, wireframing, and interaction.
