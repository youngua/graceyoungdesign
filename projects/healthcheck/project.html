    Healthcheck app helps people to order urine test kits so they can self-test in the comfort of their home, and after checking their test results, they can take in-app advice to improve their health.

    I worked alongside in a team of 3 other designers to build a MVP version of the app while focusing on the onboarding process, health, kit, and profile page.
