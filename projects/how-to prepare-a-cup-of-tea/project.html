How to prepare a cup of tea

For my Design Studio I class at RISD, I had to create a video of two minutes or less that describes "How to prepare a cup of tea" without using words or voiceovers. I had to understand how to sequence information to communicate my ideas to the audience. I was focused on how time sequencing affects the reading of the visual video.

Type: Instructional video

Date: Fall 2017

Focus: Art Direction, Narrative

Tools: Black Magic Camera, Preimere Pro
