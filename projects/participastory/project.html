ParticipaStory

The DIY character templates are characters taken from popular children's stories and illustrated onto box templates. The box templates can be colored, cut, and assembled into 3D boxes. The box templates come in three different sizes and each creates a box that can be nested in the other like a Russian doll set. The general idea is encouraging storytelling through a participatory play.
