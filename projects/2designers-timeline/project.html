2 Designers Timeline Accordian Book

Assigned to the two designers, Kazumasa Nagai and Alan Fletcher, I researched their similarities, beliefs, designs, and their life. I found Fletcher enjoys using basic primary colors while Nagai uses abstract shapes to create forms within his posters. Taking a combination of the two, I used two of the primary colors, blue and yellow as the key marker for each designer, yellow for Fletcher and blue for Nagai. I referred to origami folding techniques, as an inspiration from Nagai's use of rudimentary shapes and forms, and created a particular triangle fold for the accordion book. For Doug Scott's History of Graphic Design class, I designed an accordian book. The accordian book contains two designer's timeline and a world event timeline.

Type: Editorial Design

Duration: Fall 2017

Focus: Research & Curation

Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Lightroom
