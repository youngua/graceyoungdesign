    • 2 Designers Timeline Accordian Book

    I looked into the two designers Kazumasa Nagai and Alan Fletcher and researched their similarities, beliefs, designs, and their life. I found Fletcher enjoys using basic primary colors while Nagai uses abstract shapes to create forms within his posters. Taking a combination of the two, I used two of the primary colors, blue and yellow as the key marker for each designer, yellow for Fletcher, and blue for Nagai. I referred to origami folding techniques, as inspiration from Nagai's use of rudimentary shapes and forms, and crafted a particular triangle fold for the accordion book.
