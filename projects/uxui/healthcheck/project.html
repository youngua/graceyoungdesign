Healthcheck

The Healthcheck app is an affordable and accessible tool that allows people to self-test for early health-risk detection, response, and monitoring rather than follow traditional in-person testing procedures. With the app, people can do the following: order urine test kits, self-test in the comfort of their home, check their test results, and take in-app advice to improve their health.

For this project, I worked alongside a team of 3 other designers to build an MVP version of the app while focusing on the visual, user testing, user experience, and user interface.
