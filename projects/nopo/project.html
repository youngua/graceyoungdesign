NOPO

NOPO stands for the No Possession campaign that encourages people to own fewer things and live more effortlessly and meaningfully.

Type: Brand Identity

Date: Spring 2019

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects

Logo animation

Inspired by nested and stacked objects and minimalism movement, I imagined the "O" in the NOPO logo symbol to look like stacked hoops till the perspective changes to aerial view will then the "O" appears and completes the NOPO logo.

NOPO campaign application

Engagement piece: Daily wall calender

How do I convey my take on minimalism to people? Then I thought about what about living minimally? So I decided - what about a daily wall calendar with a daily prompt like "Today, I appreciate..." and space for people to jot down what they appreciated on that day. I want to help people declutter their minds and focus each day with something new to look forward to.

Engagement piece: Postcards

I want people to encourage their friends, families, and anyone to work towards a "No Possessions" goal. Which means to live with less but meaningful possessions and to enjoy worthful pursuits. This is a postcard of yourself that you would send off to someone else so you can inspire and encourage that individual.
