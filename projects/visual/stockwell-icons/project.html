Stockwell’s Iconography

In the summer of 2019, I was a product design intern tasked with modifying and developing Stockwell’s iconography system. The icons highlight a cherry overprint that represents action associated with each paired black outlined icon. The outlined icon emphasizes Stockwell’s goal to make the user’s experience seamless and effortless.

After finalizing the icons, I handed them off to the design and engineering team for them to apply the icons to Stockwell’s website, marketing collaterals, and mobile app.
