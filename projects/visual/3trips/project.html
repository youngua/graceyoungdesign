2020: A Young Odyssey

This 20in. by 40in. poster documents 3 different trips: one trip from my Providence living space to RISD Design Center, the second trip from my Providence living space to my hometown, and the third trip from my Providence living space to an overseas destination of my choice. As a long fan of isometric illustrations and their geometric game-like qualities, I abstracted my trips through 3d forms. In the title, 2020: A Young Odyssey, 2020 is the year the poster was created, and "A Young Odyssey" is directly inspired by the movie 2021: A Space Odyssey. "2020: A Young Odyssey" has my last name, Young, since this visualization is, after all, about my trips

This project was designed under the guidance of Doug Scott, the teacher of Mapping Information, and Weixi Zheng, an MFA student who specializes in motion graphics and infographics.
