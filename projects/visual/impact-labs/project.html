Impact Labs

Impact Labs is an organization started by two students from Brown University. Impact Labs serves as a resource for computer science students and young professionals who aim to do something impactful with their careers. The organization runs an annual winter fellowship, hosts a summer conference, and provides a coalition between students and organizations.

Impact Labs was preparing for another year to run their summer conference and 2-week winter fellowship. To gain publicity and trust, I co-lead a team to design their first brand identity and website.

I worked with Minji Koo, a RISD Alum graphic designer, and Derrek Chow and Nikhil Gupta, two UWaterloo Alum software engineers.
