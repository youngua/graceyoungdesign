    • Rip-off Poster

    Poster, not really, for an outrageous exhibition that took place at the RISD Design Center back in spring 2019. No Poster Show is where the Graphic Department juniors attempted to excite and perhaps bribe people to come to the show without making the traditional 24” x 36” medium posters that are usually hung around campus and public city places.

    Rip-off each layer until the precise location of the exhibition is revealed.
