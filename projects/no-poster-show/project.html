    • No Poster Show

    In spring 2019, an outrageous exhibition took place at the RISD Design Center. This exhibition turned out to be the No Poster Show where the Graphic Department juniors attempted to excite and perhaps bribe people to come to the show without making the traditional 24” x 36” medium posters that are usually hung around campus.

    After pounding for endless days and testing 3 other prototypes, the product I present you is the No Poster Show rip-off poster. Yes, it’s not a poster since you can tear each layer until the precise location of the exhibition is revealed.
