This is a website for a RISO printed comic organized by two graphic design students from RISD. The RISO printed comic is a collaboration of 48 RISD students across different departments. This comic started as two separate narratives, denoted by the RISO yellow & blue and pink & purple. For this setup, 24 students worked on narrative-one while the rest on narrative-two. One student joined the two separate narratives in the middle, completing the entire story. Just like a telephone game, a student completed one page of the comic, making-up the narrative and using different mediums to execute it before passing it off to the next student in line. As a participant of this collaboration, I was part of narrative-one group and illustrated page number 22.

In the summer of 2020, I worked with graphic designer Corinne Ang to develop the website.
