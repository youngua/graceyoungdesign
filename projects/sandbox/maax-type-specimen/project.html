MAAX Type Specimen

This is a type specimen animation of MAAX, a typeface licensed under 205TF and designed by Damien Gautier. The design of the typeface emphasizes geometric and elementary forms reminiscent of Bauhaus’s first typographic experiments. Based on Gautier’s inspiration, I played around with MAAX’s geometric appearance through transitions while showcasing the typeface's properties like weights, scale, punctuation, and much more.

I used Adobe After Effects for animation and Adobe Premiere for adding sound effects. The animation effects and techniques were learned in Weixi Zeng’s Motion Graphic Fall 2019 workshop.
