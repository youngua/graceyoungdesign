Physical & Digital Data Keychains

For my design studio III class advised by Jacek Mrowczyk, I created and curated an archive of images and text regarding how digital data interact with physical data. To represent the interaction of data between one physical environment to another, I handmade keychains, objects that are personal and meaningful.

Type: Product

Date: Fall 2018

Focus: Concept

Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, UV Printer, Laser Cutter
