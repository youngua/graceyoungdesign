Future Folktale

Inspired to do SOCIAL GOOD, we envision a FUTURE where we make the experience of FOLKTALE storytelling 3D for our audiences who are MUSIC PRODUCERS. In my Design Studio III class, my teammates and I were randomly assigned 5 contraints. With the 5 constraints, we had to consider and design something to do with those constraints.

Team: Tomiris Shyngyssova & Megan Peng

Type: Exhibition Design

Date: Fall 2018

Focus: Exhibition & Concept

Tools: Illustrator, Figma, Invision, Keynote

The Exhibition

We created an experimental and interactive installation

Challenge

How do we as a team work collaboratively on incorporating the five assigned constraints creatively while balancing all our vision and voice in the project?

Moodboards

After brainstorming we found and curated visuals and keywords that allow us to start imagining what our end product will feel and look like.

Idea Pitch

We pitched our ideas to our peers and teachers and in return, we received valuable feedbacks that clarify our concept and approach to the project.

Story Writing

We wrote about a robot character and it's 4 choices it makes and its 16 endings to its story.

iPad Interface

Taking the story we wrote, I prototyped an interface where users can be the robot character and select the options to find out their entire story at the end.
