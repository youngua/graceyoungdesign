Grace is a wholesome senior at the Rhode Island School of Design, from the Boston area. She studies graphic design and concentrates in Computation, Technology, and Culture.

She does information design, brand identity, UI/UX, product design, web design, and coding. But, most importantly, she is always hungry to learn!

She designed for Neoscape, Stockwell, Impact Labs, and Fuzion.

She is an elusive sagittarius that loves cooking chinese cuisine, watching historical & martial art chinese dramas, reading life-lesson books, and engaging in long-winded conversations with strangers.

As a fun project, Grace designed and coded this website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
