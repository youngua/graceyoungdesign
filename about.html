    • Hi there, Grace Young is your local graphic and product designer and illustrator based in the East Coast. She enjoys bringing weirdness into her work and is interested in cultural differences, fewer material possessions, textures, and hand-drawn things.

    She studied Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) and concentrated in Computation, Technology, and Culture.

    When she is not designing, she is usually cooking different types of cuisines, reading novels that touch upon culture & self-discovery, learning about personal finance for that FIRE life, and working on personal projects.

    Currently, she is developing a website for a client, coloring for a collaborative project, and painting digitally with friends.
